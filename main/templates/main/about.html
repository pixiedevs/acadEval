About Us

AcadEval is a web application which aims to evaluate the performance of students in their academics by considering their marks like external, internal and attendance. The fuzzy logic is employee for calculating the performance of students in an effective way. The evaluation of student’s academic domain is a significant and challenging task faced by every institution and parents for the betterment of the children life. This gives emerge to numerous software and techniques. The exams are being conducted and are maintained as record such that it could be employed for future performance evaluation. The various attributes should be taken into account for the performance analysis. The attributes involves the internal marks, external marks and attendance of the students. This attributes are evaluated by means of fuzzy logic which eliminates the usage of formulas. The summing up is performed for the evaluation of performance by this method. This system is more flexible as the fuzzy logic method allows user to implement subjectivity, uncertainty of the values if demanded by the system. The results obtained in this model are more effective when compared to other statistical methods. The performance evaluation in comparison is performed for analysing the student activity in their students. The attendance is also considered to be an important criterion in this evaluation as it also determines the performance. The data will not be lost easily and is of error free in this system.

Our Institution

Technocrats Group Of Institutions

Technocrats Group of Institutions is the oldest and largest NBA Accredited Education group in central India. It is affiliated to RGPV - Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya. The college is ranked 97th for Engineering by Times 2018. Founded in year 1999, Technocrats Group of Institutions is one of the Central India's largest and oldest Educational Group led by a team of IIT, IIM and Stanford University Alumni. Technocrats Group of Institution comprises of 4 Engineering, 1 Management, 2 Pharmacy, 1 Commerce Law and Sciences and 1 Flim and Design institutes with about 16,000+ students and 1,326+ faculty members on campus, offering courses from Undergraduate upto Doctoral level. The group has MoUs with University of Cincinnati, University of Central Missouri, Tokyo Institute of Technology for research, King Mongkut’s University of Technology, Namibia University of Science and Technology and California Baptist University, academics as well as student exchange programs. The group also has Innovation Centers of Bosch, Dassault Systems, RedHat, Oracle, AWS, Bentley, Mitsubishi Electric, Siemens, Google and Texas Instruments to prepare students for industry. The group is the largest Technical Educational Group in Central India to have NBA Accreditation.
